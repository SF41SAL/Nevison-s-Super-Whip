About Us

Nevison's Super Whip is a warm, family-run ice cream business based in Darlington, UK. With a love for tradition and community, we bring joy and nostalgia through soft whip ice cream, colourful ice lollies, premium hard scoop flavours, and our signature lemon-topped cones. Whether you spot our van on your street or book us for a celebration, our goal is simple: spread smiles, one scoop at a time.
