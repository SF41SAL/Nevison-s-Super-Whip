About Us

Nevison's Super Whip is a beloved, family-run ice cream business bringing joy across Darlington and beyond. With a history steeped in community spirit and the sweetest of memories, we specialise in soft whip ice cream, fun and fruity ice lollies, rich hard scoop flavours, and our iconic lemon-topped treats.

From casual park days to grand celebrations, we pride ourselves on quality, cheerfulness, and service that feels like a scoop of sunshine. Our colourful van is a welcome sight for kids and grown-ups alike — bringing delicious nostalgia and fresh flavours to every event.
