About Us

Welcome to Nevison's Super Whip! We’re a proud family-run business rooted in the heart of Darlington, England. With generations of passion and tradition behind us, we delight in serving premium soft whip ice cream, refreshing ice lollies, delicious hard scoop flavours, lemon-topped delights, and much more.

Whether you're young or just young at heart, our colourful van and friendly service are always a welcome treat. We believe that ice cream brings people together, and we’re dedicated to delivering happiness one scoop at a time. Look out for us in your neighbourhood or book us for your special event!

Events & Occasions

Looking to bring something sweet and memorable to your next gathering? Nevison's Super Whip offers full-service ice cream catering for all types of occasions, adding a magical, nostalgic touch to any celebration.

  • Birthday Parties – Whether it’s for kids, teens or adults, we make birthdays unforgettable with cones, sprinkles, music, and magical memories.
  • Weddings – Surprise your guests with a charming ice cream van. Elegant soft serves and joyful vibes for the most special day of your life.
  • Corporate Gatherings – Perfect for staff appreciation, office parties, customer giveaways and brand events — professional, fun, and delicious.
  • Community Events – From school fetes to charity galas and local festivals, we spread community spirit one scoop at a time.

Our friendly staff, reliable service, and irresistible menu make us a favourite wherever we go. Book us today and let us serve up smiles!
